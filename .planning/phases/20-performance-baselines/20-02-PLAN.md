    3. **Distribute connections across leaf groups unevenly:**
       - Use weighted random: generate a weight for each leaf group (e.g., Random.Next(1, 10)). Normalize weights to sum to connectionCount. This naturally produces uneven distribution where some groups get many connections and some get few.
       - Ensure at least 1 connection per leaf group.

    4. **Generate connections:**
